# Content Preferences Examples

This file contains ready-to-use content preference examples that you can copy into your Search System Prompt.

**Where to add these:** Settings > AI Assistant > Search > Search System Prompt > CONTENT PREFERENCES section

---

## E-Commerce

```
CONTENT PREFERENCES (E-Commerce)

Product Prioritization:
- For product searches, prioritize product pages over blog posts and documentation
- When users ask "how to buy" or "where to purchase", emphasize product pages and checkout process
- Comparison queries should highlight product comparison pages and feature matrices
- "Best" or "recommended" queries should lead with top-selling products and customer favorites

Category Hierarchy:
- Electronics category queries should prioritize flagship products (laptops, smartphones)
- Accessory queries should suggest compatible products and bundles
- Sale and discount queries should emphasize current promotions and clearance items

Support Content:
- Troubleshooting queries should prioritize product-specific support documentation
- Warranty and return queries should highlight our policies and contact information
```

## Usage Instructions

1. Choose the example that best matches your business type
2. Go to Settings > AI Assistant > Search
3. Scroll to "Search System Prompt"
4. Find the "CONTENT PREFERENCES" section
5. Replace the placeholder with your chosen example
6. Customize the specific terms, page names, and conditions to match your site
7. Save changes and test with relevant search queries

**Pro Tip:** You can combine multiple sections from different examples to create a custom configuration that matches your unique needs.
